Tentang

Evta Rizki Kurniawan

19670051

I am a student at PGRI University in Semarang, starting to look for a role in the world of work in the informatics industry. This project is the final project of the Geographic Information Systems course.

As an informatics major, I am fascinated by the invisible power that shapes our world, which is the visual power. The alluring visual world is certainly supported by the creator's creativity in the creation of his visual work. From the many design experiences I have, I want to develop my skills in the field of informatics. One of them is creating a web. The worlds of design and informatics are closely interconnected because a program created will produce visual output.
